Our Story

After Hurricane Maria devastated Puerto Rico in 2017, Chef José Esteban Rivero and his wife Sarah Santos made a profound decision: to return to their island home and contribute to its recovery through what they knew best—exceptional food and genuine hospitality.

In April 2019, they opened PROLE Cocina & Barra in the vibrant Calle Cerra neighborhood of Santurce. With Chef José's 11 years of training in Philadelphia and Sarah's extensive background in marketing and events, they created more than just a restaurant—they built a community gathering place.

PROLE is deeply rooted in the De la Finca a la Mesa (Farm-to-Table) philosophy. Every dish celebrates the island's agricultural resilience, featuring fresh, seasonal ingredients sourced from local Puerto Rican farmers. Our menu changes every few days, ensuring that what you taste today is the freshest and finest available.

This is our love letter to Puerto Rico—a commitment to quality, community, and the incredible bounty of our island.
